Extra rewards

Apart from your regular staking rewards, you will also receive pool rewards which we will be shared between our delegators! After reducing server costs, the pool rewards will be distributed proportionally to each delegator based on their stake. The minimum stake to receive your extra reward is 5000 ₳.

Growing pledge

As late joiners in the cardano community, we are not able to start off with an enormous pledge, but we will continue adding more ADA so it becomes more competitive.

Low fees

The aim of this pool is to support Cardano's ecosystem and it's decentralization. As soon as we start receiving blocks, the 1% fee will be enough to cover server costs, and won't be increased.

Reliable servers

Hosted on award winning cloud computing providers, with reduntant relays and distirbuted over 2 different continents, we can guarantee an uptime of more than 99.9%. This infrastructure also enables us to perform updates and do maintenance without the need to bring the nodes down.

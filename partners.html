Pool partnership

The rewards are distributed after we subtract 75 ₳ from the fixed pool costs and 10% of the pool rewards. In the table below you can find the current distribution of the pool rewards, and it's approximate earnings when just one block is minted in the epoch.


Initial Stake Shares Bonus rewards Wallet address

Rewards will be sent in the following epoch, right after the rewards are deposited in the pool wallet. Please double check your wallet address in the table above.

In case any partner reduces the stake we will reduce his/her share of the rewards. These shares won't be redistributed to other partners but will rather be used to bring more partners in the future, or to existing partners that increased their initial stake. Please note that this table wont be updated in real time.

Given the 2M ₳ stake to be active on epoch 258, we are estimating to mint blocks and generate rewards 5 out of every 6 epochs, based on the adapools.org statistics that can be found under the rewards tab. For the pool to mint blocks and generate rewards in all epochs, we will need to reach a stake of approx 3M ₳ in the pool, so your help in advertising the pool will benefit all of us.
